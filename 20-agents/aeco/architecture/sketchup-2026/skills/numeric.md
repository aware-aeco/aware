# Numeric API reference

A number of methods have been added to the Ruby Numeric class to do units conversions.

- `cm` — The cm method is used to convert from centimeters to inches.
- `degrees` — The degrees method is used to convert from degrees to radians.
- `feet` — The feet method is used to convert from feet to inches.
- `inch` — The to_l is used to convert from a number to a length.
- `km` — The km method is used to convert from kilometers to inches.
- `m` — The m method is used to convert from meters to inches.
- `mile` — The mile method is used to convert from miles to inches.
- `mm` — The mm method is used to convert from millimeters to inches.
- `radians` — The radians method is used to convert from radians to degrees.
- `to_cm` — The to_cm method is used to convert from inches to centimeters.
- `to_feet` — The to_feet method is used to convert from inches to feet.

- `to_km` — The to_km method is used to convert from inches to kilometers.
- `to_l` — The to_l is used to convert from a number to a length.
- `to_m` — The to_m method is used to convert from inches to meters.
- `to_mile` — The to_mile method is used to convert from inches to miles.

- `to_yard` — The to_yard method is used to convert from inches to yards.
- `yard` — The yard method is used to convert from yards to inches.
